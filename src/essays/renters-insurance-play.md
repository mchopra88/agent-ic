HomeEasy processes 30,000 renter leads per month. Every renter needs renters insurance. Every renter is a customer we already have. The marginal cost of offering insurance to a lead we're already talking to is near zero.

That's the thesis. Not "let's build an insurance company." Just: we already have the customer, we already have the conversation, and insurance is a natural adjacent product that the same conversational agent can sell. Different SKU, same architecture.

We partnered with a national renters insurance carrier — one of the largest in the US. The negotiation was instructive. They opened with a commission structure. I pushed for something that reflected the reality of what we were bringing: not just a referral, but an embedded distribution channel with 30,000 warm leads per month and an AI agent that could qualify, quote, and close without human intervention.

The same AI system that matches renters to apartments can present insurance options, answer questions about coverage, collect payment, and bind a policy. The renter doesn't know or care that they're talking to an insurance agent now instead of an apartment locator. The interface is the same. The conversation continues. The database changed.

This is vertical expansion as decomposition. The apartment locator decomposes into: conversational agent + apartment inventory + matching logic. The insurance agent decomposes into: conversational agent + insurance product catalog + quoting engine. The conversational agent is shared infrastructure. The inventory swaps out. The marginal cost of adding a new vertical to an existing customer base approaches zero.

The broader pattern: every business that acquires customers for one product can sell them adjacent products at near-zero marginal cost if the distribution channel is automated. This is what Amazon did with Prime — acquired customers for shopping, then sold them streaming, then groceries, then pharmacy. Same customer, new verticals, shared infrastructure.

The difference is that Amazon needed millions of dollars to build each new vertical. I need a new prompt and a new API integration.
